About the developer

Hi, I’m Andrea Sbrogio, a 22-year-old Computer Engineering Master’s student at Politecnico di Milano. I’m passionate about building complex software systems where performance optimization and parallel computing play a key role. I enjoy tackling technical challenges that require efficiency, scalability, and clean architecture.

Why Suvenir?

Suvenir started as a personal challenge to explore mobile development in depth, but after receiving strong interest and positive feedback from people who discovered the idea, I decided to turn it into a real product and publish it. Suvenir represents both my passion for technology and my desire to create something useful and meaningful for others.
